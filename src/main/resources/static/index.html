Welcome

Experience the Flavours of India

Indian Hive AB is an Indian restaurant in Sweden, dedicated to delivering the bold, vibrant, and diverse flavors of India with unmatched authenticity.

Indian Hive is redefining Indian dining by blending time-honored recipes with innovative techniques and a contemporary twist. Our meticulously crafted menu showcases a variety of dishes designed to delight every palate, including a wide range of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. We pride ourselves on sourcing the freshest local ingredients to ensure every dish is a perfect balance of flavor and quality.

Whether you're a longtime fan of Indian cuisine or a first-timer, Indian Hive offers a sensory feast that captures the soul of India with every dish. Experience the magic of authentic flavors, the artistry of our chefs, and the passion behind every plate
